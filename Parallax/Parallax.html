Welcome to Costa Rica!

Costa Rica is a rugged, rainforested Central American country with coastlines on the Caribbean and Pacific.

Tree Frogs

The vibrant tree frogs are one of the many wonders of the Costa Rican rainforests.

Sloths

Sloths are slow-moving mammals that spend most of their lives hanging upside down in trees.

Rainforests

Costa Rica's rainforests are a biodiversity hotspot, teeming with wildlife and lush vegetation.

Hummingbirds

Hummingbirds are small, colorful birds known for their incredible flight capabilities.
